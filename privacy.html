Bayou Blend — Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 18, 2025

Summary: We built Bayou Blend to be privacy‑respectful. We don’t sell data, and we only collect what’s needed to make the experience work.

What we collect

  • On‑device camera processing: AR effects run locally on your device using Snapchat’s Camera Kit. Live camera input is not sent to our servers.
  • Optional saves: If you choose to save photos or progress, we may store minimal data (e.g., image file, timestamp, basic lens state) in our cloud backend.
  • Basic diagnostics (optional): We may record error logs or aggregated usage to improve stability.

How we use data

  • Provide core functionality (apply lenses, remember collectibles/unlocks if you opt in).
  • Improve performance and fix bugs.
  • Operate our gallery or sync features if you choose to use them.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. We may use trusted processors (e.g., cloud storage/database providers) strictly to run Bayou Blend. These providers are bound by agreements and only process data on our behalf.

Third‑party services

Bayou Blend uses Snapchat Camera Kit to render AR effects. Your use of Camera Kit features may also be subject to Snapchat’s terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

  • You can use Bayou Blend without saving photos or progress.
  • You can request deletion of saved items by contacting us.
  • You may revoke camera permissions at any time in your device settings (some features will stop working).

Children

Bayou Blend is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us and we will delete it.
